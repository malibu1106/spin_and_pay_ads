Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 02, 2026

Welcome to Roulette Dealer Training. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how data is handled, including usage analytics and third-party services.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). To improve our application and understand user experience, we process data using the following methods:

  • Anonymous Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect non-identifiable usage statistics (such as screen visits and session duration). This is tracked using a local, randomly generated anonymous ID that does not identify you personally.
  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, and IP address are processed automatically by Google services.
  • In-App Purchases: Financial transactions are handled securely by Google Play Billing; we do not have access to your payment details.

2. Third-Party Services

Our application uses third-party SDKs that collect data for operational purposes:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: Securely processes in-app purchases.

3. Local Storage

Your game statistics (scores, progress, preferences) are stored exclusively locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Your Rights and Data Deletion

In accordance with Google Play policies, you have full control over your data:

  • Local Data: You can clear all game statistics by clearing the app cache or uninstalling the application.
  • Resetting Analytics: Since we use anonymous identifiers, deleting the app or clearing its local storage will reset your anonymous user ID.
  • Advertising ID: You can reset or delete your advertising ID via Settings > Google > Ads on your Android device.
